My name is Chi-Ning Chou. I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Taiwan University this (2016) June. I'm currently working as a research assistant under the supervision of Dr. Kai-Min Chung.


I'm applying for Ph.D programs in theoretical computer science this year. My research interests are in the field of complexity theory, algorithm analysis, combinatorics, optimization, and high-dimensional statistics.
